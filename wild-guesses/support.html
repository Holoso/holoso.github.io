Wild Guesses Support

Welcome to the support page for Wild Guesses! Here you can find answers to common questions and contact information for further assistance.

Common Issues and FAQs

Q: How do I save my progress?

A: You can save your progress by signing into Game Center (iOS) or Google Play Games (Android). This allows you to store your progress in iCloud or Google Drive.

Q: Can I play without a time limit?

A: Yes, in level selection, you can switch to the mode without time limits for a more relaxed experience.

Q: Is there a mode with a time limit?

A: Yes, you can choose a mode with a time limit in level selection for a more challenging experience. You can also switch to the mode without a time limit for easier gameplay.

Q: How do I unlock new levels?

A: To unlock a new level, you must complete the previous level. Levels are rated with stars based on your performance, and each level contains 10 questions.

Q: What is endless mode?

A: Endless mode provides a continuous stream of questions without levels. Your highest streak of correctly answered questions will be displayed.

Q: Can I retry a question I answered incorrectly?

A: Yes, you can retry questions if you have made progress in the level and agree to watch an ad.

Q: How do I report typos or errors in the game?

A: You can report typos directly in the app by selecting the "Report" option on a question.
